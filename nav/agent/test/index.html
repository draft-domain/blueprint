Ever Since 2007, I've Been Dedicated to Specialized Listings. Negotiating Hundreds of Short Sales, Pre-Foreclosure and Auction Date. Throughout the Years, I've Become Well-Known for My Ability to Handle Even the Most Complicated Transactions, Thanks to My Team's Unwavering Determination and Expertise.

However, What Truly Drives Me is My Passion for Helping Real Estate Agents Achieve Long-Term Financial Success and a Positive Impact on the Communities They Serve by Becoming a Listing Specialist. The Saying Goes, "You Must List to Last!" There is No Greater Satisfaction Than Empowering Real Estate Agents to Overcome Their Fears and Helping Them Navigate the Intricate World of Specialized Listings. It's the Biggest Smile on My Face When I See Their Successful Journey!

It's this Very Passion That Inspired Me to Start BluePrint Listings/BluePrint Short Sales in 2019. My Goal is to Educate and Support Real Estate Agents Across the Country Who Share My Commitment to Making a Positive Impact. This Platform Equips Like-Minded Professionals with the Knowledge, Tools, and Empathy Necessary to Navigate the Intricate World of Specialized Listings, Ultimately Allowing the Real Estate Agent to Not Only Transform the Lives in Their Communities One Transaction at a Time But Also Their Families Live Now and in the Future.
